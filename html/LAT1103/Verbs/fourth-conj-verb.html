Fourth Conjugation Verbs

List of Fourth conjugation verbs and their grammar.

Ending Pattern

  • -iō, -īre (audio, audire)

Singular Plural
1st -iō -īmus
2nd -īs -ītis
3rd. -it -iunt

Present Imperative Chart

2nd Singular Plural
stem + ī stem + īte

Present Active Infinitive Chart

Latin Form English Translation
stem + re to verb
Ex: audire to listen

Present Passive Infinitive Chart

Latin Form English Translation
stem + ri to be verbed
Ex: audiri to be heard
