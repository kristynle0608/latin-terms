Personal Pronouns

List of personal pronouns and their grammar.

Chart for 1st person - Ego, I/we

Singular Plural
Nom. ego (I) nōs (we)
Gen. meī (of me) nostrum/nostrī (of us)
Dat. mihi (to/for me) nōbīs (to/for us)
Acc. mē (me) nōs (us)
Abl. mē (by/from/with me) nōbīs (by/with/from us)

Chart for 2nd person - Tū, You

Singular Plural
Nom. tū (you) vōs (you all)
Gen. tuī (of you all) vestrum/vestrī (of you all)
Dat. tibi (to/for you) vōbīs (to/for you all)
Acc. tē (you all) vōs (you all)
Abl. tē (by/from/with you all) ōbīs (by/with/from you all)

Chart for 3rd person - Is, Ea, Id, he, she, it

Masculine Feminine Neuter
Nom. is (he, this man) ea (she, this woman) id (it, this thing)
Gen. eius (of him, his) eius (of her, her) eius (of it, its)
Dat. eī (to/for him) eī (to/for her) eī (to/for it)
Acc. eum (him) eam (her) id (it)
Abl. eō (by/from/with him) eā (by/with/from her) eō (by/from/with it)
Nom. eī, iī (they, m.) eae (they, f.) ea (they, n.)
Gen. eōrum (of them, their) eārum (of them, their) eōrum (of them, their)
Dat. eīs (to/for them) eīs (to/for them) eīs (to/for them)
Acc. eōs (them) eās (them) ea (them)
Abl. eīs (by/from/with them) eīs (by/from/with them) eīs (by/from/with them)
