List of second declension nouns and how to decline them.

Ending Patterns for First Declension Nouns

♚ Masculine
  • -us, -ī (amicus, amicī)
  • -er, -ī (ager, agrī)
♜ Neuter
  • -um, -ī (donum, donī)
