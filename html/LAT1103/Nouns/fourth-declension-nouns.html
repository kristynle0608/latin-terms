Fourth Declension Nouns

List of fourth declension nouns and how to decline them.

Gender Endings

♚ Masculine/♛ Feminine
Include a large group of AGENT NOUNS.
  • Nominative Ending: -us
  • Genitive Ending: -ūs
♜ Neuter
  • Nominative Ending: -ū
  • Genitive Ending: -ūs
