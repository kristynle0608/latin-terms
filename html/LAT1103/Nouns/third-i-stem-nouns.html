Third (Declension) I-Stem Nouns

List of third declension i-stem nouns and how to decline them.

Gender Endings

♚ Masculine/♛ Feminine
Rules to Recognize M/F i-stem.
  1. Parasyllabics with nominatives -is/-es
    • hostis, hostis, m. -> 2 syllables
    • nubes, nubis, f. -> 2 syllables
  2. Nominatives ending in -s/-x => Stem with 2 consonants
    • urbs, urbis, f.
    • nox, noctis, f.
♜ Neuter
Rules to Recognize Neuter i-stem.
  1. Nominatives ending in -al/-ar/-e
    • animal, animalis, n.
    • mare, maris, n.
