Genitives

Types of genitives.

Genitive of The Whole

Used with/after:
  • milīa
  • after the neuter nominative and accusative of certain pronouns and adjectives such as
    • aliquid
    • quid
    • multum
    • plūs
    • minus
    • satis
    • nihil
    • tantum
    • quantum
Ex: pars urbis.

part of the city.

Ex: nēmō amīcōrum meōrum.

no one of my friends.

Ex: nihil temporis.

no time.

Ex: quid cōnsiliī?

what plan?

Ex: multum bonī.

no time.

Ex: nihil certī.

what plan?
